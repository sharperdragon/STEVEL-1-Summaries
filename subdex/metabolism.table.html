Metabolic Disorders Overview
Condition Deficiency / Pathophysiology Key Features Inheritance
Phenylketonuria (PKU) Phenylalanine hydroxylase deficiency Musty odor, intellectual disability, fair skin AR
Maple Syrup Urine Disease Branched-chain α-ketoacid dehydrogenase deficiency Sweet-smelling urine, poor feeding, lethargy AR
Alkaptonuria Homogentisate oxidase deficiency Dark urine, ochronosis, arthritis AR
Ornithine Transcarbamylase Deficiency Urea cycle defect Hyperammonemia, vomiting, no acidosis X-linked
Galactosemia (Classic) Galactose-1-phosphate uridyltransferase deficiency Jaundice, hepatomegaly, cataracts, E. coli sepsis AR
Hereditary Fructose Intolerance Aldolase B deficiency Vomiting, hypoglycemia after fruit intake AR
Tay-Sachs Disease Hexosaminidase A deficiency → GM2 buildup Cherry-red spot, no hepatomegaly AR
Fabry Disease α-galactosidase A deficiency → globotriaosylceramide Angiokeratomas, neuropathy, renal failure X-linked
Gaucher Disease β-glucocerebrosidase deficiency → glucocerebroside accumulation Hepatosplenomegaly, bone crises, pancytopenia, lipid-laden macrophages AR
Niemann-Pick Disease Sphingomyelinase deficiency → sphingomyelin accumulation Progressive neurodegeneration, hepatosplenomegaly, cherry-red macula AR
Hunter Syndrome Iduronate sulfatase deficiency → dermatan sulfate, heparan sulfate accumulation Mild Hurler features, aggressive behavior, no corneal clouding X-linked
Hurler Syndrome α-L-iduronidase deficiency → dermatan sulfate, heparan sulfate accumulation Developmental delay, gargoylism, airway obstruction, corneal clouding AR
