Ornithine Transcarbamylase Deficiency Urea cycle defect Hyperammonemia, vomiting, no acidosis X-linked
Galactosemia (Classic) Galactose-1-phosphate uridyltransferase Jaundice, hepatomegaly, cataracts, E. coli sepsis A-Recessive
Hereditary Fructose Intolerance Aldolase B Vomiting, hypoglycemia after fruit intake A-Recessive
Tay-Sachs Disease Hexosaminidase A deficiency → GM2 buildup Cherry-red spot, no hepatomegaly A-Recessive
Fabry Disease α-galactosidase A deficiency → globotriaosylceramide Angiokeratomas, neuropathy, renal failure X-linked
Gaucher Disease β-glucocerebrosidase deficiency → glucocerebroside accumulation Hepatosplenomegaly, bone crises, pancytopenia, lipid-laden macrophages A-Recessive
Niemann-Pick Disease Sphingomyelinase deficiency → sphingomyelin accumulation Progressive neurodegeneration, hepatosplenomegaly, cherry-red macula A-Recessive
Hunter Syndrome Iduronate sulfatase deficiency → dermatan sulfate, heparan sulfate accumulation Mild Hurler features, aggressive behavior, no corneal clouding X-linked
Hurler Syndrome α-L-iduronidase deficiency → dermatan sulfate, heparan sulfate accumulation Developmental delay, gargoylism, airway obstruction, corneal clouding A-Recessive
Homocystinuria Cystathionine β-synthase deficiency (or impaired B6 metabolism) Marfanoid habitus, intellectual disability, thrombosis, downward lens subluxation A-Recessive
Cystinuria Defective renal PCT transporter for COLA amino acids (Cystine, Ornithine, Lysine, Arginine) Recurrent hexagonal kidney stones, positive nitroprusside test A-Recessive
Lesch-Nyhan Syndrome HGPRT deficiency → excess uric acid and purine salvage dysfunction Self-mutilation, gout, dystonia, chorea X-linked
Von Gierke Disease (Type I) Glucose-6-phosphatase Severe fasting hypoglycemia, lactic acidosis, hepatomegaly A-Recessive
Pompe Disease (Type II) Lysosomal acid α-1,4-glucosidase (acid maltase) Cardiomegaly, hypotonia, exercise intolerance A-Recessive
Cori Disease (Type III) Debranching enzyme (α-1,6-glucosidase) Milder fasting hypoglycemia, no lactic acidosis A-Recessive
McArdle Disease (Type V) Muscle glycogen phosphorylase Muscle cramps, myoglobinuria after exercise A-Recessive
MCAD Deficiency Medium-chain acyl-CoA dehydrogenase Vomiting, hypoketotic hypoglycemia, sudden death in infants A-Recessive
Refsum Disease Impaired alpha-oxidation (peroxisomal disorder) Scaly skin, ataxia, night blindness, shortening of 4th toe A-Recessive
Zellweger Syndrome Peroxisome biogenesis defect (PEX gene) Hypotonia, seizures, hepatomegaly, early death A-Recessive
Adrenoleukodystrophy Defective β-oxidation of very long-chain fatty acids (VLCFA) Adrenal crisis, progressive CNS demyelination X-linked
