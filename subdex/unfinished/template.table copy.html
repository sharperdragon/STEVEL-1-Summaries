Medications that cause hypotension & bradycardia

Medication

Distinguishing features

Beta blockers

Hypoglycemia, bronchospasm, heart block

Calcium channel blockers

Hyperglycemia

Digoxin

Hyperkalemia, "scooped" ST segments on ECG

Clonidine

Miosis, respiratory depression

Cholinergics

SLUD (Salivation, Lacrimation, Urination, and Defecation)

Magnesium

Hyporeflexia
